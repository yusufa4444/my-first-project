A Glimpse into the Past

Founded in the year 999 AD, here lies a city with a history that spans more than a thouthand years. From the time of Tsunburbura,the goddess of the temple of Dala hill,and the chief priest, Barbushe, who also live on top of Dala hill, Kano has an unbroken chain of leadership structure, and a rich history that is deeply rooted in culture and tradition which need to be explored by the world.

Traditional Delicacies

Wainar Shinkafa

A local delicacy made from white rice, and eaten with stew made from pepper and Green leaves.

Tuwan Shinkafa

Another popular dish prepared especially during festivities.It is also made from rice, and taken with different varities of stew.

Gurasa

An traditional type of bread, prepared from wheat cereals, or more recently flour. It can be taken with a stew or grounded groundnut cake and a bit of oil.
